# 🤝 Diplomacy and Alliances

> 📌 *Game as of **29 June 2026** (beta) — details may change.*

Not every problem needs a sword. From the map, you can act diplomatically against the house that rules a chosen province — building claims, alliances and obligations.

## The diplomatic toolbox

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| 📜 **Press a claim** | Begin a casus belli that matures into a reason for [[War]] |
| ⚔️ **Declare war** | Only once you hold a matured claim and meet the conditions |
| 🛡️ **Defensive / offensive pacts** | Agreements to defend or attack alongside another house |
| 🤝 **Guarantee** | Protect an independent house |
| 💍 **Marriage** | Tie a family to you by blood (see [[Marriage and Family]]) |
| 🎁 **Favours & support** | Build goodwill and influence |
| 😠 **Pressure** | Lean on a house — at the cost of their goodwill |

## Claims first, war later

The diplomatic and military paths are joined at the hip: **you must press and mature a claim before you can declare war.** A fresh claim takes a few years to ripen, then stays valid for a window before it lapses. Claims on land *inside the kingdom or duchy you head* are stronger and faster.

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[Press claim] --> B[Matures over years]
    B --> C[Valid casus belli]
    C --> D[Now war is allowed ⚔️]
```

## Alliances

A genuine **alliance** is one of the strongest cards in the game:
- 🛡️ An ally **won't be attacked by you** (and vice versa).
- ⚔️ Allies can stand together in war.
- 💍 Marriages are a classic route to a military alliance.

Lock down a powerful ally **before** you start a war — a friend on your flank can decide the campaign.

## One step at a time

Each diplomatic action against a given house can be used **once per season** — you can't spam the same lever repeatedly in a single turn. Plan your statecraft over time.

> [!tip] Diplomacy is cheaper than war
> Marriages, pacts and favours can win you what a war would cost dearly — land by inheritance, safety by alliance, influence by gifts. Exhaust the peaceful tools before drawing steel.

## Tips

- 📜 Keep a **claim maturing** so you're always ready to strike when the moment comes.
- 🤝 Secure an **ally** before a big war.
- 💍 Use **marriage** to turn a rival into a relative.
- ⏳ Spread diplomatic actions across seasons.
