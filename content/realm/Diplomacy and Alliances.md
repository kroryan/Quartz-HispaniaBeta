# Diplomacy and Alliances

> Game as of **30 June 2026** (beta). Details may change.

Not every problem needs a sword. From the map, you can act against the house that rules a selected province: pressing claims, arranging ties, making pacts and preparing wars.

## The diplomatic toolbox

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| Press a claim | Starts the legal path toward a future [[War]] |
| Declare war | Uses a matured claim, if the military conditions are met |
| Defensive or offensive pact | Creates a promise to defend or attack together |
| Guarantee | Protects an independent house |
| Marriage | Ties families by blood and can create alliances |
| Favour or support | Builds goodwill and influence |
| Pressure | Forces influence at the cost of relations |

## Claims first, war later

War requires a valid claim. A pressed claim must mature before you can use it, and once mature it remains valid for **12 years**. Claims inside your natural kingdom or duchy are usually easier to build than distant claims.

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[Press claim] --> B[Matures over time]
    B --> C[Valid for 12 years]
    C --> D[Declare war if conditions allow]
```

If the claim lapses, you must prepare again.

## When war is blocked

A valid claim is necessary, but not sufficient. You still need enough military standing, reach from your lands, a legal rank relationship and no blocking alliance with the target. The game also protects unified al-Andalus before its historical fragmentation, so the southern grand power is not meant to be conquered early by a single opportunistic claim.

See [[War]] for the full checklist.

## Alliances

An alliance is one of the strongest defensive tools in the game:

- Allies cannot be attacked by you while the alliance holds.
- Allies can add strength in war.
- Marriage is the most reliable way to bind a powerful house.
- A good ally can protect a lower-rank start long enough to climb.

Lock down a powerful ally before a major war. A friend on the flank can matter more than another regiment.

## Timing

Diplomatic actions against the same house are limited by season. You cannot spam the same lever until it works; you need to plan statecraft over time.

## Tips

- Keep at least one claim maturing before you need it.
- Use marriage to solve military weakness.
- Do not waste a 12-year claim window while your army is unready.
- Check alliances before declaring. Attacking a friend is blocked for a reason.
