# Glossary

> Game as of **30 June 2026** (beta). Details may change.

Plain-language definitions, each pointing to the page that explains it in full.

## The basics

- **Start selector** - the new campaign screen where you choose playable house, rank and faith filter. See [[Choosing Your Start]].
- **The Four Powers** - Faith authority, People, Army and Treasury: the bars that keep you in power. See [[The Four Powers]].
- **Church / Umma / Aljama** - faith-facing name for the first Power, depending on the ruler's religion. See [[Faith and Religion]].
- **Season** - about three months; one ordinary card usually passes one season. See [[Time and Your Lifespan]].
- **Reign** - the rule of one ruler, from accession to death. See [[Time and Your Lifespan]].
- **Quick reaction** - a card that resolves immediately without passing a season. See [[Making Decisions]].
- **Court neglect** - discontent from not actively governing. See [[The Royal Court]].

## Dynasty

- **Heir** - who inherits when the ruler dies. See [[Your Dynasty and Heirs]].
- **Succession law** - the rule deciding who inherits. See [[Succession Laws]].
- **Eligible heir** - a candidate allowed by law, legitimacy, faith and title rules. See [[Succession Laws]].
- **Bastard** - a child born out of wedlock; cannot normally inherit unless legitimised. See [[Bastards]].
- **Legitimise** - make a bastard a true inheriting heir. See [[Bastards]].
- **Trait** - a personality mark that affects the realm and ruler. See [[Traits and Your Character]].
- **Stress** - strain on a ruler who acts against their nature. See [[Traits and Your Character]].
- **Dread** - intimidation that deters revolts but fades. See [[Traits and Your Character]].
- **Matrilineal marriage** - a marriage where children join the wife's house. See [[Marriage and Family]].

## Court and politics

- **Council / great offices** - Chancellor, Marshal, Military Councillor, Treasurer, Financial Councillor, Chaplain or High Priest, and Spymaster. See [[Your Council]].
- **Noble house** - a powerful family you rule with and against. See [[Noble Houses and Vassals]].
- **Faction** - unhappy houses banding together with a grievance. See [[Noble Houses and Vassals]].
- **Vassal** - a house that formally answers to you. See [[Crown Authority and Tyranny]].
- **Crown authority** - how much tax and troops vassals owe. See [[Crown Authority and Tyranny]].
- **Tyranny** - the penalty for ruling lawlessly. See [[Crown Authority and Tyranny]].
- **Hook** - leverage that forces compliance without normal tyranny costs. See [[Intrigue and Schemes]].

## Realm and war

- **Barony / county / duchy / kingdom** - the core title ladder. See [[The Map of Hispania]] and [[Climbing the Ladder]].
- **Hisn / cora / wilaya / emirate** - Muslim-flavoured names for the same rank ladder. See [[Climbing the Ladder]].
- **Claim / casus belli** - legal pretext required before war. See [[War]].
- **Claim window** - the mature period of a claim, currently 12 years. See [[Diplomacy and Alliances]].
- **War scope** - county, duchy or kingdom: how much a war is fought over. See [[War]].
- **War score** - the hidden measure of who is winning; decisive wars resolve around 70. See [[War]].
- **Civil war** - rebellion inside your own realm. See [[War]].
- **Men-at-arms** - professional spearmen or heavy foot. See [[Armies and Men-at-Arms]].
- **Alliance** - a partner you cannot attack and who can fight beside you. See [[Diplomacy and Alliances]].
- **Taifas** - fragmented Muslim statelets that appear after al-Andalus breaks apart. See [[The Map of Hispania]].

## Money and faith

- **Gold vs. Treasury bar** - your real money versus its health reflected on the Power bar. See [[Economy and Gold]].
- **Insolvency** - crippling debt that can topple the ruler. See [[Economy and Gold]].
- **Conversion** - spreading your faith in lands you control. See [[Faith and Religion]].
- **Doctrine** - a permanent religious choice bought with faith authority. See [[Doctrines and Excommunication]].
- **Excommunication** - the Pope's sanction on an impious Christian ruler. See [[Doctrines and Excommunication]].
- **Papal audience** - a Christian-only request to Rome, limited to one per season. See [[The Papacy]].

## The long game

- **Innovation** - a permanent cultural advance. See [[Culture and Innovations]].
- **Renown and legacy** - permanent dynasty-wide rewards. See [[Dynasty Legacy]].
- **Relic** - a prestige treasure won through events. See [[Relics and Treasures]].
- **Crisis** - a Power at a breaking point. See [[Crises and Disasters]].
- **Sandbox victory** - control every county on the map with your house before the era ends. See [[Winning and Losing]].
- **Granada milestone** - an important historical campaign marker, no longer the only victory condition. See [[Winning and Losing]].
