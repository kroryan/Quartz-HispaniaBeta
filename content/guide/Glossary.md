# 📖 Glossary

> 📌 *Game as of **29 June 2026** (beta) — details may change.*

Plain-language definitions, each pointing to the page that explains it in full.

## The basics
- **The Four Powers** — Church, People, Army, Treasury: the bars that keep you in power. → [[The Four Powers]]
- **Season** — about three months; one ordinary card passes one season. → [[Time and Your Lifespan]]
- **Reign** — the rule of one monarch, from coronation to death. → [[Time and Your Lifespan]]
- **Quick reaction** — a card that resolves on the spot without passing time. → [[Making Decisions]]
- **Court neglect** — discontent from not actively governing. → [[The Royal Court]]

## Dynasty
- **Heir** — who inherits the throne when your monarch dies. → [[Your Dynasty and Heirs]]
- **Succession law** — the rule deciding who inherits. → [[Succession Laws]]
- **Bastard** — a child born out of wedlock; can't inherit unless legitimised. → [[Bastards]]
- **Legitimise** — make a bastard a true, inheriting heir. → [[Bastards]]
- **Trait** — a personality mark that affects the realm and the ruler. → [[Traits and Your Character]]
- **Stress** — the strain on a ruler who acts against their nature. → [[Traits and Your Character]]
- **Dread** — intimidation that deters revolts but fades. → [[Traits and Your Character]]
- **Matrilineal marriage** — children join the wife's house. → [[Marriage and Family]]

## Court & politics
- **Council / great offices** — Chancellor, Marshal, Steward, Spymaster, Chaplain. → [[Your Council]]
- **Noble house** — a powerful family you rule with and against. → [[Noble Houses and Vassals]]
- **Faction** — unhappy houses banding together with a grievance. → [[Noble Houses and Vassals]]
- **Vassal** — a house that formally answers to you. → [[Crown Authority and Tyranny]]
- **Crown authority** — how much tax and troops your vassals owe. → [[Crown Authority and Tyranny]]
- **Tyranny** — the penalty for ruling lawlessly. → [[Crown Authority and Tyranny]]
- **Hook** — leverage that forces a house to comply without tyranny. → [[Intrigue and Schemes]]

## Realm & war
- **County / duchy / kingdom** — the tiers of land and titles. → [[The Map of Hispania]]
- **Barony** — a small holding inside a county. → [[The Map of Hispania]]
- **Claim (casus belli)** — the legal pretext you need before war. → [[War]]
- **War scope** — county, duchy or kingdom: how much a war is fought over. → [[War]]
- **War score** — the hidden measure of who's winning a war. → [[War]]
- **Civil war** — a rebellion inside your own realm. → [[War]]
- **Levies vs. men-at-arms** — mass feudal troops vs. professional regiments. → [[Armies and Men-at-Arms]]
- **Alliance** — a partner you can't attack and who can fight beside you. → [[Diplomacy and Alliances]]
- **Taifas** — the fragmented Muslim statelets that form when al-Andalus breaks apart. → [[The Map of Hispania]]

## Money & faith
- **Gold vs. Treasury bar** — your real money vs. its health reflected on the bar. → [[Economy and Gold]]
- **Insolvency** — crippling debt that can topple you. → [[Economy and Gold]]
- **Conversion** — spreading your faith in lands you control. → [[Faith and Religion]]
- **Doctrine** — a permanent religious choice bought with Church standing. → [[Doctrines and Excommunication]]
- **Excommunication** — the Pope's sanction on an impious Christian ruler. → [[Doctrines and Excommunication]]

## The long game
- **Innovation** — a permanent cultural advance. → [[Culture and Innovations]]
- **Renown & legacy** — permanent dynasty-wide rewards. → [[Dynasty Legacy]]
- **Relic** — a prestige treasure won through events. → [[Relics and Treasures]]
- **Crisis** — a Power at a breaking point. → [[Crises and Disasters]]
